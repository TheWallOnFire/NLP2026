## 🌟 Overview

The **Sign Language Detector App** is a sophisticated computer vision tool, offline mobile app, designed to recognize and interpret alphabet hand signs. Whether you're looking to learn sign language, test your skills, or perform real-time translation, this app provides a seamless and interactive experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 Vision & Detection
- **Real-Time Detection (Default):** Instant hand sign recognition via live camera feed with results displayed dynamically below the preview.
- **Image Processing:** Capture photos or upload existing images from your gallery to detect hand signs.
- **Video Analysis:** Record clips or upload videos for sequential sign detection.
- **Gallery Integration:** Directly browse and select media from your device's native gallery apps.
- **Live Pack Switching:** Switch between different sign language packs (e.g., ASL to BSL) directly from the camera preview for instant multi-dialect detection.

### 🎓 Learning & Testing
- **Dynamic Sign Library:** A curated collection of hand sign reference images that automatically updates based on your installed model packs.
- **Language-Specific Segregation:** The library and learning modes are organized by dialect, ensuring you only see reference images corresponding to the currently selected language pack.
- **Interactive Learning Mode:**
    - **Random Word Challenges:** Practice signing words generated by the app.
    - **Live Feedback:** Instant "Correct/Incorrect" validation with guidance on how to improve.
    - **Skeletal Overlay:** Real-time hand skeleton visualization to help you match the correct hand posture.
    - **Performance Analytics:** Detailed summary of your accuracy and speed at the end of each session.
- **Test Mode:** A gamified "typing game" experience—but played entirely with your hands!

### ⚙️ Customization & Flexibility
- **Model Portability:** No hard-coded models! Download or purchase new model files and load them directly via settings.
- **Model Pack Manager:** A unified interface to manage "Language Bundles" which include:
    - **Detection Models:** The AI weights for specific sign languages.
    - **Visual Reference Collections:** A set of example images for every word/character in that dialect.
    - **Automatic Synchronization:** Loading a pack instantly updates your Library and Learning pages with the correct visual assets.
- **Theme Engine:** Full support for Light, Dark, and Mixed modes with customizable color palettes.
- **Vision Settings:**
    - **Sensitivity Control:** Adjust the confidence threshold for sign recognition.
    - **Camera Selection:** Toggle between front and back cameras.
    - **Skeletal Toggle:** Enable or disable the real-time hand landmark overlay.
- **Audio & Accessibility:**
    - **Sound & Haptics:** Customize vibration intensity and sound effects.
    - **TTS Configuration:** Select system voices and adjust the speech rate for sign announcements.
- **Data & Storage:**
    - **Custom Folders:** Define where to save recorded media and progress logs.
    - **History Management:** Toggle local logging and export learning data as CSV.

---

### 📱 Mobile & Offline Optimization
- **On-Device Edge AI:** Fully optimized for offline use with ultra-low latency, ensuring your data never leaves the device.
- **Haptic Feedback:** Tactile vibrations to confirm correct signs, providing a more immersive learning experience.
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS):** Integrated system voice to announce detected characters or words aloud in real-time.
- **Offline Sign Packs:** Downloadable dialect packs (ASL, BSL, etc.) to switch between sign languages without an internet connection.
- **Battery-Aware Processing:** Intelligent frame-rate adjustment to maximize battery life during extended practice sessions.
- **Privacy-First Architecture:** Zero cloud dependency—no images, videos, or hand data ever leave your device.
- **Quick-Access Widget:** Start detecting or practicing instantly with a home screen shortcut.

### 📈 Local Progress & SRS
- **Smart History:** Tracks your learning journey and accuracy trends entirely on local storage.
- **Spaced Repetition (SRS):** An offline "Daily Review" system that intelligently picks signs you find difficult to practice.
- **Personalized Vocabulary:** Create and save custom word lists to master specific phrases.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights

- **Dynamic Model Loading:** Decoupled architecture allowing users to swap detection models on the fly.
- **Modular Model Packs:** A standardized format for sign language data, enabling easy community-made or official dialect updates.
- **Skeleton Tracking:** Uses advanced hand landmarks for high-precision feedback in learning mode.
- **Cross-Platform Potential:** Designed with mobile-first gallery and camera interactions in mind.
